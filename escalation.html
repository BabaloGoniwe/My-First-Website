Escalation Procedure

  1. Initiate a detailed report, including all troubleshooting steps taken.
  2. The support team assesses priority based on urgency and impact.
  3. High-priority issues receive responses within 2 hours; lower-priority issues may take up to 24 hours.
  4. Unresolved issues are escalated to senior technical support.
  5. Documentation of the escalation is maintained and shared as needed.
